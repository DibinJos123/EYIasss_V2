Connected SCM (Supply Chain Management)

EY opschain based blockchain solution for tracking supply chain transactions between buyer and seller through enhanced visibility and near real time tracking of product movement.

EY has developed a blockchain-based goods tracking and connected SCM platform addressing a wide range of needs for frictionless collaboration among SC players. EY’s solution is a shared, distributed platform where all SC players’ legacy/existing systems can easily integrate. Supply chain participants will be onboarded onto the blockchain platform, along with their respective business rules, and get their own nodes. The platform allows different systems and ERPs of SC players to interact with single shared blockchain ledger and securely auto-share data. This permits data to be shared and reconciled in near real-time and lets SC managers get a near real-time updated view of what stage in the manufacturing or delivery lifecycle the goods are currently in, at any given point of time.

The blockchain layer also provides data immutability, verification and increased data integrity and security. The tokenization feature of blockchain technology will be leveraged to create digital twins of physical goods and assign them unique and consistent addresses for traceability and activity logs at the lowest divisible goods-level across the supply chain. The end result derived from the verified data is customized dashboards and analytics for each SC player with updated views of their customer’s and vendor’s activities and actionable insights into where delays and bottlenecks exist in the process, to undertake targeted expediting.

Features

  • Developed using Quorum/Ethereum blockchain platform
  • Custom UI enabling SCM input/output transactions
  • Integrated dashboard to track quantity mass balance for order material in near real-time
  • Master data management mapping seller and buyer material codes
  • Traceability of goods in different stages of the post order delivery lifecycle, along with enhanced supply chain oversight
  • View on pending user actions – call out bottlenecks and take immediate actions to expedite process and check project cost and time overrun

Benefits
