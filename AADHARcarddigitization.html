AADHAR card digitization

AADHAR card digitization is the automation of existing manual and paper-based processes, enabled by the digitization of information. The details in AADHAR card are extracted in a structured format.

EY’s AI based Aadhar card digitization solution helps to reduce cost and time that puts in collecting demographic details of aadhar card document. Our solution helps to digitize aadhar card document by extracting unstructured demographic text from it and convert it into structured data that will help in verification process of various organization. Aadhar card digitization solution is generic to different formats of aadhar card. Even address of the citizen can also be extracted if it is on the back side of aadhar card. Our single solution has this capability to identify both front and back side of aadhar document.
