Blockchain Loyalty Solution platform

EY Blockchain Loyalty Solution platform enables seamless loyalty points management among stakeholders, secure real-time transactions with increased accuracy, intelligent insights and connected ecosystem with better customer experience

EY has developed a royalty points management solution that provides EY core customer focus consulting offering and emerging technologies blockchain and analytics. It provides seamless interoperability amount multiple royalty programs in a single wallet powered by blockchain to elevate the overall customer solution. This solution allows Realtime transactions and Transparency for both B2B and B2C businesses. Solution can be customised for:

  • mall Businesses looking to introduce royalty program for their customers.
  • Large Companies looking to maintain existing royalty program while creating interoperability among its respective businesses.
  • Aggregators looking to create a connected royalty ecosystem.

The platform leverages data visualization and data analytics to provide intelligent insights and help augment brand royalty and enhance the customer experience. This EY royalty program addresses all conjures of the royalty management lifecycle including

  • Royalty strategy and governance
  • Trust
  • Program design and implementation
  • Partner management
  • Program Technology Enablement and support.

Features

  • Increased transparency amongst stakeholders and frictionless partner network
  • Automatic stakeholder reconciliations and seamless liability management
  • Interoperability amongst multiple loyalty programs driving redemption rates
  • Single tokens and wallet to manage multiple participating loyalty programs
  • Secure real time accrual and redemption of loyalty points across programs
  • Peer to peer transactions with no minimum threshold limits
  • Personalized loyalty programs providing deeper insights to stakeholders
  • Intelligent insights and assurance to stakeholders

Benefits

  • Increase sales through customer retention and new customer acquisition
  • Reduce operational costs via targeted marketing and merchandise optimization
  • Increase customer satisfaction through greater engagement and enhanced experience
  • Boost brand with brand extension and advocacy from the wider network and social proof
  • Utilize vital customer insights to drive business strategy and growth decisions
